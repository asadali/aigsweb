What is AIGS?



AIGS strives to keep the Indianness running in the veins of Graduate Students from India. We create an opportunity for networking amongst the alumni and the current as well as the incoming batch. We do a few cultural gigs too. Check out our Gallery for photos of Diwali and Holi @ UCSD
